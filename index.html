Marcelo Saguas Iacovone

Rigger/Animator

After exploring animation since the age of 11, I started my formal studies in animation at Vancouver Film School, where I did my first short animated film. I then returned home to Brazil to continue my studies. I acquired my Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design from the Armando Alvares Penteado Foundation (FAAP), and made my second short animated film. Later on, I moved back to Vancouver, which is where I currently live and work. After 3 years of working as an Animator in Vancouver, I slowly developed a love for the more technical side of things and, for that reason, I then decided to migrate to rigging.

Some of my work and studies can be found in my blog.
